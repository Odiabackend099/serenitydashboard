## Common Questions

**Q: Do I need to speak the entire message at once?**
A: No, you can record, stop, then record again to add more.

**Q: Can I edit the text after speaking?**
A: Yes! The transcript appears in the input field where you can edit it like normal text.

**Q: What if it gets my words wrong?**
A: Just edit the text in the input field before sending.

**Q: Does this work on mobile?**
A: Yes! Works great on iPhone (Safari) and Android (Chrome).

**Q: Is my voice recorded or stored?**
A: No. Only the text transcript is saved. Your voice audio is processed locally by your browser and never stored.

**Q: What if I don't see the microphone button?**
A: Your browser may not support this feature. Try using Chrome or Safari.
